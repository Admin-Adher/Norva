# Norva Plans Explained: How to Compare Features Before You Subscribe

> **In short:** Choose between Norva and Norva Family primarily by how many separate household profiles you need. Norva includes up to two profiles; Norva Family includes up to five. Then verify current prices, trial conditions, billing route, cancellation terms, and feature details on the official pages before subscribing.

Profile capacity is a concrete distinction. It should not be stretched into claims about device limits or simultaneous playback. The best plan is the smallest current plan that supports the verified household workflow, subject to its complete terms.

## Start with people, not devices

A profile is useful when one person needs separate progress, history, favourites, or preferences. Count distinct viewing contexts:

- individual household members who want their own progress;
- different language or subtitle preferences;
- separate favourites and discovery history;
- adults who do not want a shared continue-watching row.

Do not create a profile for every screen. The same profile can support continuity across supported devices. Conversely, two people sharing one TV may still benefit from separate profiles.

The [household profile guide](/blog/individual-household-media-profiles/) helps decide where separation adds value.

## Compare the verified profile capacities

| Plan | Current stated profile capacity | Likely fit to test |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Norva | Up to 2 profiles | One or two distinct preference contexts |
| Norva Family | Up to 5 profiles | Three to five distinct preference contexts |

This table describes profile capacity only. It does not establish how many devices can be registered or how many sessions can play at once. Check the current official pricing and terms for any other commercial condition.

## Review the complete current offer

Before deciding, open the official pricing page and record:

1. current monthly and annual prices;
2. trial length and whether payment details are required;
3. renewal timing;
4. taxes or regional conditions shown;
5. cancellation route for the platform used to subscribe;
6. refund terms that apply to that billing provider;
7. current feature and profile descriptions.

Avoid relying on an article screenshot or remembered price. Commercial terms can change, and billing rules can depend on the provider used to subscribe.

The [guide to what Norva includes](/blog/what-norva-includes/) separates software features from the authorised source, rights, devices, network, and storage you still provide.

## Test profile separation during the trial

If a trial is currently available under the stated terms, create only the profiles you truly need. On each:

- mark a different favourite;
- create a playback checkpoint on a known item;
- choose a distinct language preference if available;
- reopen the profile on another supported device;
- verify that one profile's state does not replace another's intended state.

Use representative items from your own compatible source. Available tracks and recommendations depend on that source. The [Norva trial checklist](/blog/norva-trial-checklist/) covers setup, search, playback, TV, and cross-device continuity as well.

## Decide by scenario

**Choose Norva for further evaluation** when no more than two people or preference contexts need separation.

**Choose Norva Family for further evaluation** when three to five distinct profiles are a real household requirement.

**Pause the decision** when source compatibility, device support, rights, or a critical workflow remains unverified. A larger plan cannot solve an incompatible source or missing media track.

## Common mistakes and limitations

- Counting screens instead of distinct profile needs.
- Inferring simultaneous playback from profile capacity.
- Choosing a plan before testing the compatible source.
- Relying on an old price in a saved article.
- Assuming every media item supports preferred languages.
- Treating Norva Family as a parental-control product.
- Forgetting that cancellation and refund routes can depend on the billing provider.

Recheck current pricing, terms, and feature pages immediately before purchase.

## Frequently asked questions

### Is Norva Family only for families?

The name describes a plan with up to five profiles. Evaluate it whenever a household needs three to five distinct profile contexts; do not infer unlisted controls from the name.

### Do profiles equal devices?

No. A profile holds a person's viewing context. The stated profile capacity does not establish a device limit.

### Should I choose annual billing immediately?

Compare the current commercial terms and first verify the workflow during any available trial. The right billing period depends on your decision and the terms shown at that time.
