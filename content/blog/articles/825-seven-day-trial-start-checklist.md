# A Checklist Before Starting the Seven-Day Trial

> **In short:** Norva currently advertises a seven-day free trial that requires a payment method and becomes the selected paid plan unless canceled before the trial ends. Before confirming, record the exact start and end, displayed price, currency, taxes, renewal date, reminder method, original provider, and cancellation path. Verify profile capacity, supported device, authorized compatible source, and offline conditions. Recheck the final purchase screen because offers and provider rules can change.

A trial is useful when it tests real household needs rather than creating an untracked renewal. This checklist does not promise refunds, cancellation timing, or consumer-law outcomes; use current provider terms and qualified advice where needed.

## Confirm the official offer

Open Norva's current official site or application listing. Verify that the trial is available for your account and purchase channel, because eligibility can vary. Record the offer URL, retrieval date, and provider.

Norva's current landing page states seven days, a payment-method requirement, automatic transition to the selected paid plan unless canceled, a reminder before renewal, and possible taxes. Human review must confirm every statement before publication.

## Select the plan before the clock starts

Count recurring people who need separate profiles. Current Norva material states up to two profiles for Norva and up to five for Norva Family. Use the [plan-by-profile guide](/blog/choose-plan-by-profile-needs/) and [Norva-versus-Family framework](/blog/norva-vs-norva-family-decision-framework/).

Do not select from an assumed device or simultaneous-playback difference. Only current published terms can support such a distinction.

## Capture exact commercial details

On the final screen, record the displayed plan name, price, billing interval, currency, tax statement, trial start, trial end, first paid-renewal date, and provider. If the screen shows a reminder method or timing, record the exact wording.

Do not save complete card details, store credentials, transaction tokens, or full receipts in the checklist. A redacted confirmation reference can be stored securely if needed.

## Verify the cancellation route now

Before starting, locate cancellation controls for the original provider and understand whether cancellation leaves access until the end of the trial or billing period. Do not assume account deletion, sign-out, or uninstall cancels the subscription.

Norva's current terms direct subscription management to the original store or payment provider. Save the official help URL and verify status after any cancellation action.

## Prepare the account and source

Secure the sign-in email, create a unique Norva password, and confirm the recovery route. Identify a compatible source you own or are authorized to use and secure that source independently.

The [pre-account checklist](/blog/prepare-before-creating-norva-account/) covers these tasks without recording credentials. A trial cannot meaningfully test browsing or playback if source readiness is unresolved.

## Prepare a supported device

Confirm current support for the television, mobile device, or browser you will actually use. Check operating-system version, application availability, network, storage, and screen-lock or household controls.

If offline viewing is a decision factor, verify that the device, source, media, storage, and rights are eligible. Do not assume every item can be stored offline.

## Define trial success criteria

Choose a small set of tests: account creation, source connection, catalog organization, profile separation, cross-device continuity on supported devices, playback, subtitles or audio selection where available, and conditional offline behavior. Define what would make the plan useful before the trial begins.

Avoid testing with unauthorized sources or exposing credentials in screenshots and support requests.

## Schedule two checkpoints

Set a mid-trial review to check setup and a decision checkpoint before the stated end. Calendar reminders are personal safeguards and do not replace any provider reminder. Allow enough time to follow official cancellation instructions and verify status.

The [media-account lifecycle handbook](/blog/media-account-lifecycle-handbook/) explains what to retain if you continue and what layers to close if you stop.

## Common mistakes and limitations

- Starting before recording the exact trial end.
- Assuming the payment method will never be charged.
- Forgetting taxes, currency, or billing interval.
- Choosing a plan from device count rather than profiles.
- Treating uninstall as cancellation.
- Depending only on a provider reminder.
- Testing unsupported devices or unauthorized sources.

## Frequently asked questions

### Does the trial currently require a payment method?

Norva's current offer says yes; verify the final purchase screen and original provider terms before confirming.

### What happens after the seven days?

The current offer says the selected paid plan begins unless canceled; verify exact price, timing, tax, and provider conditions live.

### Does deleting the app cancel the trial?

Do not assume so. Use the original provider's cancellation control and verify the resulting subscription status.
