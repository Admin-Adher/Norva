# Record Your Offline Library Before Changing Devices

> **In short:** Before replacing, resetting, or uninstalling on a device, list every offline item, exact version, intended profile, required tracks, and progress state. Reconnect and verify account sync first. Follow the platform’s official transfer or backup process, but do not assume app-managed local copies will migrate. On the new device, recheck eligibility and prepare needed items again.

An offline library is easy to overlook during migration because it sits inside app-managed device storage. Account state and local media are different layers and may not follow the same transfer path.

## Separate what can sync from what is local

Norva can sync account information such as progress, history, favourites, and preferences across supported devices. Norva's privacy policy states that eligible offline items are encrypted and stored on the device and are not uploaded to Norva.

That distinction means a restored account can show your catalogue and progress without proving that a playable local copy exists on the destination. The guide to [where offline downloads live](/blog/where-offline-downloads-live/) maps these layers in detail.

## Inventory before changing anything

For each local item, record:

- title, season, episode, and version;
- intended account and profile;
- current progress or completion state;
- required audio and subtitle tracks;
- observed local size;
- reason it is still needed;
- date of the most recent disconnected test.

Use screenshots only if they do not expose sensitive account or source information. A written table is often sufficient.

## Reconcile account state first

While the old device remains available, connect it through a trusted network and verify progress using the [first-reconnection sequence](/blog/plan-first-reconnection-after-offline/). Check the same profile on another supported device only after the old device's intended state is visible.

Do not erase, reset, or uninstall until this check and the inventory are complete. Norva states that downloaded media is removed when the user deletes it or uninstalls the app.

## Follow official platform migration guidance

Apple documents several device-transfer methods and recommends having a backup before beginning. Android also documents backup and device-copy workflows, while noting that not every app can back up or restore all settings and data.

These platform tools do not establish that a specific Norva offline item will transfer. Treat the inventory as the source for what must be rechecked, not as a promise that files will reappear.

The broader [moving Norva to a new device checklist](/blog/move-norva-to-new-device/) covers account, source, and trusted-device steps beyond offline storage.

## Rebuild only what is still needed

On the destination device:

1. complete official device setup;
2. sign in through the normal Norva path;
3. confirm the intended profile and catalogue;
4. compare synced progress with the inventory;
5. check free local storage;
6. verify current item-level offline eligibility;
7. prepare only the items still needed;
8. test each item without connectivity.

Offline availability remains conditional on the new device, compatible source, media, and associated rights. A copy that worked on the old device does not prove current availability on the new one.

## Common mistakes and limitations

- Assuming a device backup includes every app-managed local item.
- Erasing the old device before progress reconnects.
- Recording titles without versions or episode numbers.
- Forgetting required audio and subtitles.
- Preparing the entire old batch again without reviewing need.
- Assuming old-device eligibility carries to the new device.
- Storing passwords or source credentials in the inventory.

## Frequently asked questions

### Will offline items transfer with my phone backup?

Do not assume so. Platform backup coverage varies, and offline items are app-managed local data. Inventory them and verify the destination independently.

### Should I keep the old device until everything is checked?

When practical, keep it unchanged until account state and essential new-device items are verified. Follow the platform's official security and trade-in guidance.

### Can I copy the encrypted files manually?

Do not manipulate app-managed files unless official Norva instructions explicitly support it. Recheck eligibility and use the app's normal offline controls.
